🏢 Sobre a empresa ISA ENERGIA PN N1

ISA Energía Brasil S.A. engages in the electric power transmission business in Brazil. As of December 31, 2024, it had a total installed transformation capacity of 85,000 MVA together with transmission lines of 23, 0000 kilometers; and 136 substations. It operates in the states of Alagoas, Bahia, Espírito Santo, Goiás, Maranhão, Mato Grosso, Mato Grosso do Sul, Minas Gerais, Paraíba, Paraná, Pernambuco, Piauí, Rio de Janeiro, Rio Grande do Sul, Rondônia, Santa Catarina, São Paulo, and Tocantins. ISA Energía Brasil S.A. was incorporated in 1999 and is headquartered in São Paulo, Brazil.
